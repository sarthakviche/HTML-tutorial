About CSI 

 
The Computer Society of India (CSI) is a non-profit organization that represents computer professionals in India: 
 Founded: In 1965 by a group of IT enthusiasts 
 Mission: To promote research, knowledge sharing, and career enhancement for IT professionals 
 Activities: Includes monthly technical meetings, product presentations, exhibitions, film and video shows, and publications 
 Membership: Requires members to abide by the CSI Code of Ethics 
 Chapters: CSI has chapters across India, including in Agra, Allahabad, Chandigarh, Dehradun, Delhi, and more 
 Student branches: CSI has student branches across India 
 Publications: CSI publishes CSI Journal of Computer Science and Informatics, CSI Communications, and CSI Adhyayan
