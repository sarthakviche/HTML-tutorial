Purpose 

The CSI's purpose is to advance the theory and practice of computer science

Mission 

 
The CSI's mission is to facilitate research, knowledge sharing, learning, and career enhancement for IT professionals. 
Activities
The CSI's activities include student branches, lecture meetings, seminars, conferences, training programs, programming contests, and practical visits. 
Recognition
The CSI recognizes innovations and indigenous developments in India's information technology field. It also awards individuals and organizations working in the ICT field. 
Structure
The CSI is guided by an Executive Committee, whose members are elected by voting members of the Society. The President is the functional head of the Society, and is assisted by the Vice President, Secretary, and Treasurer.
